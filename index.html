Who we Support


As COVID-19 made its rounds around the world, many K-12 schools shutdown. The National School Lunch Program was previously able to provide ~30 million students affordable school lunches daily but, now that students are staying at home, many parents need cost-effective ways to get meals to their kids.

Feeding America is one of the largest charities in the United States. They run over 200+ food banks and feed ~50 million people annually! They need our help more than ever during these tough times.

Quarantine4Kids is a 100% free program but, we strongly suggest that parents consider contributing $5 / class to Feeding America via our GoFundMe campaign
